-+ {{item.name}} Acrostics: Like acronyms of words, but give word meanings to help you remember. Anagrams: A word combination of the first letters of a list of words you want to remember. Celebrity Numbers: A way to remember a sequence of 2 numbers by having letters representing numbers a=1,b=2...i=9,j=10 and having those letters represent the first letters of a celebrity's first and last names. Mnemonics: A list remembered as a sentence where, the first letters of the words of the sentence are the same as the first letters of the words to remember. Peglist: A list remember by associating the index of the list(a number) with a word associated with the number(usual using major system) to help you remember.
